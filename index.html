1. Information We Collect

Habitoid does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data including your habits, completion history, streaks, goals, achievements, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using local storage mechanisms.

We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Access your contacts, location, or other device data
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or advertising services

2. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. This includes:

  • Habits: Your habit names, categories, frequencies, and completion history
  • Progress Data: Streaks, completion statistics, and achievement progress
  • Goals: Your personal goals and target dates
  • Settings: App preferences and notification settings

We do not have access to your data, and it never leaves your device. You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or using the app's reset functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Habitoid does not integrate with any third-party services that collect or share your data. Specifically, we do not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • User tracking services
  • Cloud storage services
  • Social media integrations

All functionality is provided through the app itself, and all data remains on your device.

These features are completely optional and under your control. The export/import process happens entirely on your device, and we do not have access to exported or imported data.

5. Children's Privacy

Habitoid is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms
  • We recommend using device lock screens and keeping your device updated
  • Data is not transmitted over the internet, eliminating network-based security risks
  • No servers or cloud storage are involved, so there's no risk of data breaches on our end

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete: You can delete individual habits or reset all data through app settings
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all your data from the device
